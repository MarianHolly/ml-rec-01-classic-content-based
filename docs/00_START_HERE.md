### üìä Project Summary

**What You'll Build**: 
A content-based movie recommendation system that suggests 10 similar movies based on genres and user-generated tags.

**Technologies**: 
Python, pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, matplotlib, seaborn, Jupyter

**Dataset**: 
MovieLens 25M (62,000 movies with genres and tags)

**Time Commitment**: 
20-30 hours over 2-3 weeks (10 hours/week)

**Key Algorithms**:
- TF-IDF vectorization for text features
- Cosine similarity for finding similar movies
- Precision@K for evaluation

**Expected Outcome**: 
A professional Jupyter notebook with precision@10 ‚â• 0.7, complete with visualizations, evaluation, and documentation ready for your portfolio.
